Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our online tools

How do I compress an image without losing quality?

Our image compressor uses advanced algorithms to reduce file size while maintaining visual quality. Simply upload your image, adjust the compression level if needed, and download the optimized version. The tool supports JPG, PNG, and WebP formats.

Are these tools completely free to use?

Yes, all our basic tools are 100% free with no hidden charges. We offer premium features for power users, but the core functionality of image compression, PDF conversion, and other essential tools remains completely free.

Is my data secure when using your online tools?

Absolutely. We process all files directly in your browser using client-side technology. Your files are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security. We don't store or access any of your documents.

What is the maximum file size I can process?

Our free tools support files up to 100MB. For larger files, we offer premium plans that support files up to 1GB. The file size limit depends on your browser's capabilities and available memory.

Can I use these tools on my mobile device?

Yes, our website is fully responsive and works perfectly on all mobile devices. You can compress images, convert PDFs, and use all our tools directly from your smartphone or tablet browser.

What image formats does your converter support?

Our image converter supports all popular formats including JPG, PNG, WebP, BMP, TIFF, and GIF. You can convert between any of these formats while maintaining image quality.
