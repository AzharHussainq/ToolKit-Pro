Privacy Policy

At ToolKit Pro, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Welcome to ToolKit Pro's Privacy Policy. This document outlines how we handle your information when you use our website and services. By using ToolKit Pro, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. The types of information we collect include:

1.1. Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password.
  • Content You Submit: Files you upload for processing through our tools.
  • Communications: Information you provide when contacting our support team.

1.2. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you use our website, including tools accessed, time spent, and features used.
  • Device Information: Information about your device, including hardware model, operating system, and browser type.
  • Log Information: Details about how you've used our services, including device event information and IP address.

1.3. Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third-party services, such as when you choose to sign in using Google or other authentication services.

Note About File Processing

ToolKit Pro processes your files directly in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly choose to save them to your account. For most tools, processing happens entirely on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Purpose Description
Provide Services To deliver the tools and features you request
Improve Our Services To understand how our services are used and enhance user experience
Communicate With You To send service-related announcements and respond to your inquiries
Personalization To customize content and remember your preferences
Security To protect against fraud, abuse, and security risks

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to analyze trends, administer the website, track users' movements around the site, and to gather demographic information about our user base.

3.1. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the operation of our website
  • Analytical/Performance Cookies: Allow us to recognize and count visitors
  • Functionality Cookies: Enable us to personalize content
  • Targeting Cookies: Record your visit and pages you have visited

3.2. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: We will share personal information with third parties when we have your consent to do so.
  • Service Providers: We work with third-party service providers who help us operate our services.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which we collected it, including to satisfy any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

For files processed through our tools, we typically do not store your files on our servers. Processed files are available for immediate download and are automatically removed from our systems after a short period.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Portability: Request access to or a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Restriction and Objection: Request restriction of or object to our processing of your personal data.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent at any time where we rely on consent to process your personal data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at azarhussin44@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than the country in which you reside. These countries may have data protection laws that are different from the laws of your country.

We take appropriate safeguards to require that your personal information will remain protected in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the security of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
