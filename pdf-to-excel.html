Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PDF to Excel conversion

How accurate is the PDF to Excel conversion?

Our PDF to Excel converter uses advanced OCR technology to achieve up to 99% accuracy in data extraction. It preserves formatting, recognizes tables, and maintains data integrity during the conversion process.

Can I convert scanned PDF documents to Excel?

Yes, our tool supports OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology that can extract text and data from scanned PDF documents and convert them into editable Excel spreadsheets.

Is there a file size limit for conversion?

You can convert PDF files up to 100MB in size. For larger files, we recommend using our desktop application or splitting the PDF into smaller sections.

How long does it take to convert a PDF to Excel?

Conversion time depends on the file size and complexity. Most files are converted within seconds, while larger files with complex layouts may take a few minutes.

Will my data be kept private during conversion?

Yes, we take privacy seriously. Your files are encrypted during transfer and automatically deleted from our servers after conversion. We do not store or access your documents.
