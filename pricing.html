Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our pricing and support options

Are the tools really free to use?

Yes, all our tools are completely free to use with no hidden costs. You can access all features without any payment. The support options are entirely optional for those who wish to contribute to the development and maintenance of ToolKit Pro.

What will my support be used for?

Your support helps cover server costs, development time, and maintenance of the website. It also allows us to create new tools and improve existing ones. We're committed to keeping the core functionality free while using support funds to enhance the platform.

Is there a minimum amount for support?

For USDT (BSC), the minimum is $1 equivalent. For EasyPaisa and JazzCash, you can send any amount you're comfortable with. Every contribution, no matter how small, is appreciated and helps us continue providing free tools.

Will I get premium features if I support?

Currently, all features are available for free. In the future, we may add premium features for supporters, but the core tools will always remain free. Current supporters will receive early access to new tools and priority support.

How do I know my support was received?

For cryptocurrency transactions, you can check the transaction status on the blockchain explorer. For EasyPaisa and JazzCash, you'll receive a confirmation message from your provider. If you'd like us to confirm receipt, you can contact us with details of your transaction.
